HOW TO BUY YETI?

Get a Wallet

Download and install a crypto wallet like MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet, which will serve as your digital key to the crypto world. Make sure to securely store your seed phrase, as it's the only way to recover your wallet.

Go to Uniswap

Visit the Uniswap platform, where you'll be able to swap your ETH for YETI tokens. Ensure your wallet is connected and on the Base network for a smooth transaction.

Connect to Base

Switch your wallet to the Base network by adding it in the wallet’s network settings or through a Base-supported dApp. This connects you to the ecosystem where Yeti resides and operates.

Swap for YETI

On Uniswap, connect your wallet and exchange your ETH for YETI tokens. You’re now officially part of the YETI adventure—welcome to the community!
